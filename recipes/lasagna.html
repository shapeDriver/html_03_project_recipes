Lasagna

Lasagna made with pre-boiled pasta plates, bologna sauce and béchamele.

This classic pasta casserole is sure to delight both family and friends as a hearty meal that will satisfy all hungers, with only basic cooking skills needed.

Ingredients

  1. 12 lasagna plates
  2. 500g minced meat
  3. 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  4. 60g of smoked pork
  5. 1 carrot
  6. 1 onion
  7. 2 cloves of garlic
  8. 2 tbsp. of olive oil
  9. 3 tsp. Provençal spices
  10. 2 pinches of nutmeg
  11. 1 tsp. of salt
  12. 1 pinch of black pepper
  13. 2 tsp. honey
  14. 8 dl of milk
  15. 5 tbsp of flour
  16. 5 tbsp. of butter
  17. 1.5 dl. of parmesan
