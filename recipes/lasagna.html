Lasagna made with parboiled pasta plates, bologna sauce and béchamele.

This classic pasta casserole is sure to delight both family and friends as a hearty meal that will satisfy all hungers, with only basic cooking skills needed.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna plates
  • 500g minced meat
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 60g of smoked pork
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 3 tsp Provençal spices
  • 2 pinches of nutmeg
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 pinch of black pepper
  • 1 dl of red wine
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 8 dl of milk
  • 5 tbsp of flour
  • 5 tbsp of butter
  • 1.5 dl of parmesan

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 275 deg C. Fry minced meat and smoked pork untill properly browned. Cut onions and carrot into small diced cubes and fry in the olive oil for 10 min. Add crushed garlic at the last minute. Once softened, add tomato, spices, salt and cook the sauce with lid on for 15 min, then reduce without lid for 20 min. Add the honey and red wine and cook for 5 further minutes. Set aside for the assembly.
  2. Make a roux of butter and flour. Then add half of the milk, whisk untill nicely blended and add the rest. Cook untill thickened and add the parmesan and nutmeg. Set aside.
  3. Add some cheese sauce at the bottom of the pan, then cycle plates, bolognese and cheese sauce. Top the last plates with cheese sauce and some extra parmesan. Put in the oven for 15 minutes, then lower the temperature to 225 deg. C and cook 15 minutes more. Let the lasagna rest and serve with a nice sallad.
