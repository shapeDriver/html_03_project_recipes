Basic swedish dinner

A lazy and easy dinner for worn-out mondays!

Ingredients

  • Some cooking oil
  • 400 g of Falu sausage
  • 500 g of potatoes
  • (Optional) Garnish such as parsley

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes for 25 minutes.
  2. Fry the sausages in slices
  3. Plate the sausage slices and boiled potatoes and garnish optionally with parsley.
