Creamy potato and leek soup

This soup is great on cold autumn nights to warm hearts and guts!

Ingredients

  • 400 g potatoes
  • 1 leek
  • 5 dl milk
  • 1 dl cream
  • 1 l chicken broth
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch of white pepper
  • 3 crushed allspice corns
  • A few chives for garnish
  • Some cooking oil for frying

Steps

  1. Boil the roughly diced potatoes in broth and milk in a pot , then fry the leek that has been sliced in a frying pan.
  2. Add the fried leek into the soup pot untill melded beautifully.
  3. Add the seasoning, then blend the soup until smooth.
  4. Serve in deep dishes and garnish with chives.
