Sausage Stroganoff

Another swedish template dinner! Sure to delight your friends!

Ingredients

  • 400 g Falu sausage
  • Butter for frying
  • 1 onion
  • 1 390 g can of crushed tomatoes
  • A few parsley pieces for garnish
  • 3 dl jasmin rice
  • 2 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp chinese mushroom soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. Fry the sausage cut into staffs, then set aside on a plate.
  3. Fry the diced onion untill soft.
  4. Add the rest of the ingredients except garnish and cook for 30 minutes
  5. Plate the dish and garnish with chopped parsley. Serve with peas and/or cucumber-tomato sallad.
