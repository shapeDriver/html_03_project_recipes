Bullens pilsnerkorv

A classic swedish summer lunch dish, simple but delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Bullens pilsnerkorv
  • 1 bay leaf
  • A few peppercorns
  • 8 hot dog buns
  • Mustard
  • Pickle relish

Steps

  1. Heaten up the Bullens can contents
  2. Toast the buns
  3. Serve the hot dogs with condiments and a smile!
